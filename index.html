About Me

Hi, my name is Tyler and I am a Sophomore at Michigan State University. I am originally from the Greater Boston Area in Massachusetts. During my studies in College and High School, I have developed experience with Java and Python through doing a variety of projects in each language.

During the Summer of 2017 I was a front end development intern in Boston at Getfused. This was where I got the chance to build my skills with CSS and HTML. In addition to my portfolio (this website) I am planning on continuing web development while studying for my Computer Science degree.

When I am not coding or studying, I like to be outside with friends. Whether it is my Intramural Softball team or skiing with friends in Maine, I enjoy being active.
